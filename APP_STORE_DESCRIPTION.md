# StreamUp — App Store Description

## App Name
StreamUp

## Subtitle
Movies & TV Shows on Apple TV

## Description

StreamUp is the ultimate companion for movie and TV show lovers on Apple TV. Browse, discover, and track everything you watch — all from your couch with the Siri Remote.

**DISCOVER NEW FAVORITES**
Explore trending, popular, and top-rated movies and TV shows powered by TMDB and TVDB. A cinematic home screen with spotlight hero banners and beautifully designed carousels makes browsing feel like an experience, not a chore.

**AI-POWERED RECOMMENDATIONS**
Get personalized suggestions tailored to your taste. StreamUp uses AI to analyze your watch history and surface content you'll actually want to watch — no endless scrolling required.

**TRACK YOUR WATCHING**
Sign in with Trakt to sync your watch history, manage your watchlist, and pick up right where you left off with Continue Watching. Your data stays in sync across all your devices.

**STREAM YOUR WAY**
Add your own streaming sources with Stremio addon support. StreamUp fetches streams from multiple sources simultaneously and lets you pick the best one — all with a gorgeous stream selection interface.

**RICH DETAIL PAGES**
Dive deep into any movie or show with full cast info, ratings, genres, seasons, episode guides, and more. Ambient color backdrops adapt to the content you're viewing for an immersive feel.

**BUILT FOR APPLE TV**
Every pixel is designed for the big screen. Smooth focus animations, parallax motion, ambient artwork, and a collapsible sidebar make navigation feel native and effortless.

**KEY FEATURES**
• Trending, popular, top-rated, and now-playing carousels
• AI-powered personalized recommendations
• Trakt integration — watchlist, history, ratings, and Continue Watching
• Stremio addon support for streaming from your own sources
• Full movie and TV show details with cast, seasons, and episodes
• Universal search across all content
• YouTube trailer buttons on detail pages
• Customizable carousel ordering
• Ambient color backdrops that match focused content
• Kids profile filtering
• iCloud sync for preferences
• SharePlay support

**REQUIREMENTS**
• Trakt account (free) for watchlist and history sync
• Internet connection for browsing and streaming
